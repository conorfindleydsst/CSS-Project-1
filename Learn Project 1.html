The Pros and Cons of Conrad Johnson

First of all, Conrad has longer legs that the Rock. Also, he has a black stripe belt in karate, so he has very quick feet. All of these could possibly help Conrad beat the Rock.

However, Conrad is also lacking in many areas. For one, Conrad is a toothpick compared to the log that is Dwayn Johnson. Also, Conrad doesn't weigh nearly as much as The Rock. Conrad is only 6 foot 4 compared to the Rock's 6 feet 5 inches. Conrad is also lacking in the experience area, as the Rock was a WWE superstar.

What Conrad Has to do to Have a Chance

  • Use his long legs
  • Avoid strong attacks
  • Cry
